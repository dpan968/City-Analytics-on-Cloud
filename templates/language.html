Language and Twitter

Discovery


The data has shown a positive correlation between the rate of non-English twitter and rate of non-English speaking population over a sample period. For instance, NSW, with 786k non-English speaking population and corresponding 200k non-English tweets, is leading on both rankings. In a percentage prospective, NSW donates the highest percentage of non-English tweets (weights 50% of total tweets) with 32% of total non-English speaking population.Each state’s stats can be found by the following interactive bar charts and radar charts.
